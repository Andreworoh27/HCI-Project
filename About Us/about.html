Our History
Tanaka guitar was founded in 2006 in Jogjakarta to develop selling and
support many guitarist around the world. Tanaka guitar provide best
quality guitar around the world and for the best guitarist in the world. In
2009, we expand our store to various location such as, Banyuwangi,
Balikpapan, and Jakarta.
We sell various type of guitar such as classic guitar, electric guitar, bass
guitar, acoustic guitar, and many more. we colaborate with some high
quality brand such as Yamaha, Seagull, and Gibson. these brand offers high
quality guitar. In 2016, we open our online shop via website and some e-
commerce partners to expand our bussiness widely.
